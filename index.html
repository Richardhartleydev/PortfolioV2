ABOUT ME

Who is Richard?

A curiosity-driven developer looking for a challenge.

I'm a passionate frontend developer dedicated to crafting user-friendly web experiences. Committed to staying at the forefront of industry trends to create seamless and visually appealing websites - Turning creative ideas into interactive realities.

I have a Bachelors Degree in Biomedical Science from Liverpool John Moores University in the UK, and have completed the Meta front-end developer professional certificate.
Currently working for the UK's largest gym operator Puregym.

I'm happiest when creating experiences from the ground up, including this website! I love solving real world problems. Feel free to reach out just to say Hello. Everyone is welcome!
